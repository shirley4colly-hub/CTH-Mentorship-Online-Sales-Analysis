# CTH-Mentorship-Online-Sales-Analysis
Project Overview:
This repository contains an end-to-end analysis of sales transactions for an online retail company. Using Microsoft Excel for data modeling and visualization, and PowerPoint for storytelling, the project delivers actionable insights to improve sales, optimize inventory, and enhance customer experience.

Objectives: 
Identify sales trends across regions, products, and time.

Detect top-performing products and underperforming areas.

Create effective dashboards and visualizations.

Communicate findings with clarity, accuracy, and business relevance.

Data Description:
The dataset includes transaction-level details which includes

Product Description, Category, Country, Payment Method

Sales Channel, Return Status, Shipment Provider, Warehouse Location

Order Priority, Invoice ID, Stock Code, Invoice Date, Year, Month, Quarter, Time

Customer ID, Discount, Shipping Cost, Quantity, Unit Price, Sale Price, Profit

Key Measures (KPIs):
Shipment Frequency → Count of shipments by provider.

Total Sales  → Sum of Sale Price.

Total Quantity Sold → Ranking by sales volume.

Number of payment methods → Distinct count of payment methods

Total Stockcode → Distinct count of Stockcode.

Dashboards & Visualizations:
Regional Sales Trends (2020–2025) – Line chart showing performance by country.

Sales by Product Category – Bar chart comparing category revenues.

Product-Level Performance – Horizontal bar chart of top product descriptions.

Hourly Sales Trend – Line chart identifying peak sales hours.

Shipment Frequency Distribution – Bar chart comparing courier volumes.

Order Priority by Payment Method – Bar chart showing customer behavior.

KPI Summary Dashboard – Total Sales, Quantity Sold, Payment Methods, Stock Codes.

Actionable Recommendations is not limited to the following:
1. Improve Sales & Marketing:
Focus campaigns on high-growth regions (UK, US).

Promote Accessories & Furniture categories.

Target peak sales hours (2 PM, 11 PM) with ads.

2. Optimize Inventory & Stock Planning:
Increase stock for top-performing products.

Reduce or reposition underperforming SKUs.

Align warehouse stock with regional demand.

3. Enhance Customer Experience:
Strengthen partnerships with FedEx (highest shipment frequency).

Improve PayPal checkout experience for high-priority customers.

Expand payment options beyond the current three methods.

Conclusion:
This analysis provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, operational efficiency, and customer behavior. By implementing the recommendations, management can drive growth, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
